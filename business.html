Countering extremism in the workplace

How should I prepare my business?

The ProtectUK website has specific content catered towards ensuring your business is doing its best in countering radicalisation. Recommended steps include:

  1. View the latest advice and guidance available on websites such as ProtectUK, which have helpful sections regarding your specific needs. If interested in looking at how to ensure your business keeps moving, check out Response. Looking for ways to protect your staff and prepare them? Have a look at the Security section. If there are any specific types of guidance that you are looking for that you cannot currently find on our site, please use the feedback form
  2. Perform a risk assessment, helping you understand and identify what potential risks exist in your business.
  3. Get interactive by performing ACT awareness e-learning to bolster your security knowledge. If you have questions around security or want to increase your knowledge, start a discussion with employees or speak to other business professionals on key topics.
  4. Read news articlesthat give insight into the latest updates on the world of counter terrorism and threat analysis.
  5. Sign up to webinars organised by ProtectUK. They continue to update the range of offered webinars, allowing you to get live or pre-recorded advice from authoritative voices within the industry, helping you stay on top of the latest in counter terrorism and security.
